Percy Jackson and the Olympians

Short Introduction:

Percy Jackson & the Olympians is a popular young adult fantasy series by Rick Riordan about a troubled boy named Percy Jackson who discovers he is a demigod. The series is set in the modern United States and blends Greek mythology with contemporary life, following Percy and his demigod friends as they navigate Camp Half-Blood and embark on quests to protect the world from mythological monsters and ancient gods.
(Source: Goodreads)
