Heroes of Olympus

Short Introduction:

The Heroes of Olympus is Rick Riordan's sequel series to the Percy Jackson and the Olympians series. It focuses on Greco-Roman mythology instead of only Greek mythology. The novel details a conflict between Greek demigods, Roman demigods, and Gaea.
